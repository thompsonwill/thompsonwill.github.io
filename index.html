About Me
Will was born in and briefly lived in Atlanta, GA before moving to Kennett Square, PA. Briefly. He lived there for another year before moving to Chicago where he spent the next 12 years until moving to North Carolina.

After moving once again, he completed High School and enrolled at Johnson & Wales University where he received his bachelors degree in Entrepreneurship and Marketing Management. After graduation, he moved back to Chicago where he started a career in sales.

His passion has always been technology, which is why he enrolled to Northwestern's Coding Bootcamp. He is excited to begin a career in web development and learning all he can!
